About Us

Al-Tarek is a Syrian-based company specializing in supplying, installing, and maintaining advanced medical imaging equipment. With over 20 years of experience, we serve the public and private healthcare sectors with cutting-edge radiology systems, integration services, and dependable technical support.

Our Services

  • Exclusive local representative for global manufacturers
  • Equipment import on-demand with no upfront payment
  • Installation, operation, and user training
  • System integration (DICOM, HIS, RIS, PACS)
  • Software and hardware troubleshooting
  • Preventive & corrective maintenance contracts
  • Medical exhibitions and marketing support
  • Regulatory and import license assistance

Devices We Work With

  • MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging)
  • CT Scanners (Computed Tomography)
  • Digital X-ray Systems
  • Mammography Machines
  • Ultrasound Imaging
  • Cardiac Catheterization Labs
